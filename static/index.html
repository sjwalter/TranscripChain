Welcome to TranscripChain!

TranscripChain is a game. To play, you call 1-888-232-2698 (toll free). You will hear a computer read you a transcription of what the last person to call in said. Then, after the beep, you say back to the computer exactly what you heard. The computer will record what you say and transcribe it, and that transcription will be read to the next person who calls in.

This may sound complex, but it's not. See for yourself by calling 1-888-232-2698.
